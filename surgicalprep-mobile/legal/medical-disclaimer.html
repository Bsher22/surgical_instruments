Important Notice

SurgicalPrep is designed for educational purposes only.

This application and its content are intended to supplement professional education and training, not replace it. The information provided should never be used as the sole basis for clinical decision-making in patient care.

Educational Purpose Only

SurgicalPrep is an educational tool designed to help surgical technologists, operating room nurses, and students learn about surgical instruments and organize preference cards. The app is intended to:

  • Supplement formal education and training programs
  • Provide a study aid for certification exam preparation
  • Help users familiarize themselves with surgical instruments
  • Assist with organizing and studying preference cards

Not a Substitute For

SurgicalPrep is NOT intended to replace:

  • Professional Medical Training: Accredited surgical technology programs, clinical rotations, and continuing education courses
  • Institutional Protocols: Your facility's policies, procedures, and guidelines for surgical instrument handling and sterilization
  • Manufacturer Instructions: Specific instructions for use (IFU) provided by instrument and equipment manufacturers
  • Professional Supervision: Direct oversight and guidance from qualified surgical professionals, preceptors, and supervisors
  • Clinical Judgment: The professional judgment of licensed and certified healthcare professionals

Limitations of Content

While we strive to provide accurate and up-to-date information, please be aware that:

  • Medical knowledge and best practices evolve continuously
  • Instrument usage may vary between facilities, surgeons, and procedures
  • Regional and institutional differences exist in terminology and practices
  • The app may contain errors or omissions despite our best efforts
  • Images may not represent all variations of instruments
